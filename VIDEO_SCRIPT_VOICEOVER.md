## Part 2: The Problem (10-20 seconds)

Traditional blockchain games expose all data publicly. Every card played, every bet made, and every strategic decision is recorded on-chain for everyone to see. ## Part 3: The Solution (20-35 seconds)

FHEVM makes this possible. Our Privacy Poker implementation uses Fully Homomorphic Encryption to keep all game data encrypted. Players can make moves, compare hands, and manage bets—all while their information stays completely confidential. The blockchain verifies everything without ever seeing the actual data.
